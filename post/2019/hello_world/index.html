Buddha’s words of Dhamma:

Paṇḍito sīlasampanno jalaṃ aggīva bhāsati; bhoge saṃharamānassa bhamarasseva irīyato.

Dīgha Nikāya 3.265

English translation:

One who is virtuous and wise shines forth like a blazing fire; like a bee collecting nectar one acquires wealth by harming none.

Sharing a wonderful documentary: https://www.amazon.com/Queen-Sun-What-Bees-Telling/dp/B00715QPXI/
